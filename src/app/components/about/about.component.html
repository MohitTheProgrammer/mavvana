About

Mavvana is a name that's been taking the music world by storm, captivating audiences globally with his electrifying beats and innovative soundscapes. As a celebrated DJ and producer, Mavvana has become synonymous with genre-defying music, effortlessly blending elements of trap, bass, and moombahton into a distinctive style that has set dancefloors ablaze worldwide.

Hailing from India, this dynamic artist has rapidly risen to prominence, earning acclaim for his unparalleled talent and high-energy performances. His debut single "Guineo," featuring Godwonder & Rebel of You, was a game-changer, marking the beginning of a trailblazing career that continues to push the boundaries of electronic music. With an ever-growing fanbase and a reputation for delivering unforgettable live shows, Mavvana is undeniably one of the most exciting names in contemporary music, and his influence is only set to expand.
